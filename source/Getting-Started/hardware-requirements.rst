************************
Typical Miniscope System
************************

A typical miniscope data acquisition system based on the UCLA Miniscope design includes :doc:`/Hardware-Guide/data-acq-hardware` and :doc:`/Software-Guide/index` in addition to the miniscope itself. Optional :doc:`/Hardware-Guide/accessories` include:

*   An :ref:`Hardware-Guide/accessories:Open Ephys Coaxial Commutator` to prevent twisting of wired connections between a freely-moving animal and a stationary data acquisition system  

*   A :ref:`Hardware-Guide/accessories:MiniCAM` is to record animal movement
